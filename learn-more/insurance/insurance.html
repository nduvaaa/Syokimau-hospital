Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my insurance is accepted?

You can check our list of accepted insurance providers on this page or contact our billing department directly at +254 113 566 500. We are constantly expanding our network, so it's best to verify even if you don't see your provider listed.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your insurance card, a valid photo ID, any necessary referral forms, and payment for any known co-pays or deductibles. If you have any questions about your specific requirements, please call ahead.

How does the billing process work?

After your visit, we'll submit a claim to your insurance provider. Once processed, they will send an Explanation of Benefits (EOB) detailing what was covered. We will then bill you for any remaining balance. If you have questions about your bill, our billing department is available to assist you.

What if I don't have insurance?

We offer various payment options and financial assistance programs for uninsured patients. Our financial counselors can work with you to find the best solution for your situation, including payment plans and potential discounts.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans to help make your medical expenses more manageable. Our financial counselors will work with you to set up a plan that fits your budget. Contact our billing office to discuss your options.

Need Assistance?

Our dedicated team is here to help you navigate insurance matters and financial concerns. Don't hesitate to reach out with any questions about coverage, billing, or payment options.
