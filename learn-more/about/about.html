About Us

Syokimau Hospital is a nonprofit academic healthcare organization serving the community with excellence in patient care, research, and medical education. Founded in 1995, we've grown from a small community clinic to a comprehensive medical center with state-of-the-art technology and expert healthcare professionals. Our commitment to compassionate care, innovation, and community health has made us one of the most trusted healthcare institutions in Kenya.

Quality Care

We are committed to providing the highest standard of medical care to all our patients, combining advanced technology with compassionate service to meet your healthcare needs.

Experienced Team

Our team consists of highly skilled doctors, nurses, and support staff dedicated to your health and well-being, with specialized training in various medical disciplines.

Modern Facilities

We offer state-of-the-art medical facilities and equipment to ensure accurate diagnoses and effective treatments, maintaining the highest standards of safety and hygiene.

Our Mission

At Syokimau Hospital, our mission is to provide exceptional healthcare services with compassion and integrity. We are dedicated to improving the health and wellbeing of the communities we serve through excellence in patient care, education, research, and community engagement.

We strive to be accessible to all members of our community, regardless of background or socioeconomic status, and to address the unique healthcare challenges facing our region.

Our Vision

To be the premier healthcare provider in Kenya, recognized for our clinical excellence, patient-centered approach, and innovative medical practices. We aim to lead in medical advancement while maintaining our core values of compassion, integrity, and service.

By 2030, we envision Syokimau Hospital as a center of healthcare excellence in East Africa, setting the standard for patient care, medical research, and healthcare education across the region.

Our History

2025

Specialized Departments

We opened our first specialized departments, including cardiology, pediatrics, and obstetrics, enhancing our service offering.

2025

Major Upgrade

Syokimau Hospital grew into a full-service medical facility with over 150 beds and state-of-the-art medical equipment.

2025

Research Center

Launched our medical research center focusing on tropical diseases and public health challenges in Kenya.

2025

Today

Today, we stand as a beacon of healthcare excellence, serving thousands of patients annually with a team of over 500 healthcare professionals.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all aspects of patient care and service delivery, constantly seeking to improve our standards and outcomes.

Compassion

We provide care with kindness, empathy, and respect for the dignity of each person, recognizing the human aspect of healthcare.

Integrity

We adhere to high ethical standards and demonstrate honesty in all actions, building trust with our patients and community.

Teamwork

We value collaboration among our staff, with our patients, and with the community, recognizing that the best outcomes come from working together.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas, technologies, and approaches to improve healthcare delivery and patient outcomes.

Community

We are committed to serving and improving the health of our community through outreach, education, and accessible care.
