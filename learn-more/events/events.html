How to Register

Registration Details

  • Click the "Register Now" button on the event you want to attend
  • Fill out the registration form with your details
  • You'll receive a confirmation email with event details
  • For virtual classes, a link will be sent to you before the class begins

Additional Information

  • Most classes are free for our patients
  • Some specialized classes may have a small fee
  • Please arrive 15 minutes early for in-person events
  • Contact us at events@syokimauhospital.com for any questions
