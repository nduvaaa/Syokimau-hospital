Parking Information

Patient & Visitor Parking: Free parking is available in lots A, B, and C.

Handicap Parking: Designated spaces available in all lots closest to main entrances.

Getting to Syokimau Hospital

Public Transportation

  • Bus: Routes 103, 107, and 110 stop directly in front of the hospital.
  • Matatu: Routes 35 and 45 pass within a 5-minute walk to the hospital.

Driving Directions

  • From Nairobi CBD: Take Mombasa Road south for 15 km. Exit at Syokimau and follow signs to the hospital.
  • From JKIA: Head south on Airport North Road, then west on Eastern Bypass. Turn south on Mombasa Road and follow signs to Syokimau.
  • From Machakos: Take Mombasa Road north toward Nairobi. Exit at Syokimau junction.
