Excellence in Cardiac Care

At Med Oasis Hospital's Cardiology Department, we are committed to providing comprehensive heart care using the most advanced diagnostic and treatment methods available. Our team of cardiologists, cardiac surgeons, and specialized healthcare professionals work together to deliver personalized care for all types of heart conditions.

From prevention and early detection to advanced treatment and rehabilitation, we offer a complete continuum of cardiac care to help our patients lead healthier lives with stronger hearts.

Our Cardiac Services

Comprehensive heart care for all your needs

Diagnostic Services

Advanced cardiac diagnostics including echocardiography, stress testing, Holter monitoring, cardiac CT, and cardiac MRI.

Interventional Cardiology

Minimally invasive procedures including angioplasty, stent placement, and other catheter-based treatments.

Cardiac Surgery

Surgical procedures including coronary bypass, valve repair and replacement, and treatment of congenital heart defects.

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Personalized rehab programs to help patients recover from heart attacks, surgery, and other cardiac events.

Electrophysiology

Diagnosis and treatment of heart rhythm disorders, including pacemaker and defibrillator implantation.

Preventive Cardiology

Risk assessment, lifestyle modifications, and medical interventions to prevent heart disease.
